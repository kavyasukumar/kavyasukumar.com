I am a developer-journalist interested in using my technical skills in finding and presenting stories.

I was a software developer at Microsoft when the journalism bug bit me. Now armed with a master's degree in reporting from Medill, Northwestern Unversity, I am a Data Interactive Editor at the Palm Beach Post.

My work involves sifting through large quantities of data to find stories and creating data services and visualizations to help tell the story. I also do custom digital presentations for longform stories. And every once in a while, I report and write my own stories.
